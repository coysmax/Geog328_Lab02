History

The Boston Red Sox are a professional baseball team based in Boston, Massachusetts. Founded in 1901, the team has a storied history including multiple World Series championships and legendary players.

Historic moments

  • 1903: Early AL play and first championships.
  • 2004: Ended the 'Curse of the Bambino' with a World Series victory.
  • 2007, 2013, 2018: Additional World Series titles.

FAQ

The franchise was established in 1901 as one of the American League's eight charter franchises.

The team name came from the red hose (socks) that were part of the early uniform; the modern spelling 'Red Sox' became common in the early 20th century.
